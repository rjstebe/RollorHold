Score Options
5pts
2 Pairs is when there are a pair of dice that share a value and a second pair of dice that share a value. It is worth 5 points.
10pts
3-of-a-kind is when three or more dice have the same value. It is worth 10 points.
20pts
Small Straight is when there are four or more dice with consecutive values. It is worth 20 points.
25pts
Flush is when all of the dice values are odd, or all of the dice values are even. It is worth 25 points.
30pts
Full House is when there are three dice that have the same value, plus a pair of dice that share a value. It is worth 30 points.
40pts
4-of-a-kind is when four or more dice have the same value. It is worth 40 points.
50pts
Large Straight is when all five dice's values are consecutive. It is worth 50 points.
50pts
Yam-Slam is when all five dice have the same value. It lets the player pick any remaining or discarded point chip and take an extra turn. It is always available.
